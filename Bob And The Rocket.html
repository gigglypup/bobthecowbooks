Bob and the Rocket
By Nathan J. Greway
Illustrations By Nathan J. Greway
Once upon a time, in that town where Bob and Joe lived,
lived Bob and Joe. One day, Bob saw a truck driving down
the street. On it, it said RSEG-Random Space Explorer
Guys but Bob didn't think much of it. Ding-Dong! Ding-
Dong! The doorbell rang! Joe ran downstairs to open it, but
there was no one. All there was was a big cardboard box
large enough to sleep in. And that's just what Joe wanted
to do. Bob didn't want to be left out, so he curled up with
Joe in the box that night. It was still outside, as it was too
big to fit through the door.
In the morning, before anybody else was up, the truck
came back. Bob and Joe were still deep in DreamLand,
while the RSEG truck took the box and loaded it up! What
Joe hadn't realized the night before was that the box had
writing on it just like the truck! It said, Please Load
Supplies For Moon Expedition-Thank You. As the truck
came to the RSEG headquarters, Bob started to come
around. He tried to get out of the box, but it was no use.
It was taped shut! The box was loaded onto a space shuttle
without them knowing, (for by then, Joe had woken up) and
they were out to space! The countdown timer began...
10...9...8... Bob and Joe were still in the box...
7...6...5...4...3... They were scared now! 2...1... LIFTOFF! The
rocket sped out of the Earth at a million miles an hour! The
sudden jolt knocked the breath out of their lungs!
Meanwhile, they were going to the moon...
Bob and Joe both fell asleep after that, even after a full
nights sleep, but they were awoken by the sudden cold
blast of air. They were out of the rocket and into outer
space! Oops, the rocket must have sprang a leak in the
cargo hold. Too dangerous to land. Gotta go back They
heard right before everything got quiet.
They awoke from their faint hours later, and Bob cut the
tape on the box. They were on the moon! They started out
exploring, but they were hungry. They couldn't walk any
farther. Both were desperate for edible things, until Bob
glanced at the ground. It was kinda squishy, so he managed
to break off a piece (it is very hard to break off things
with hooves, if you've ever tried) and tasted it. That was
when he made the biggest discovery since Neil Armstrong
actually discovered this place. THE MOON IS MADE OF
GREEN CHEESE!!!! He shouted, and he and Joe dug in.
After they had had their fill, Joe tried to call RSEG HQ
on his cell phone, only to find they didn't have service.
What kind of moon is it that doesn't have cell
service???!!! Yelled Bob. But they decided to make the
best of their situation. Joe had an idea. Let's make this
moon like a luxury vacation! With all this easy-moldable
cheese, we can create the luxury hotel of our dreams! So
they did. Joe lived in the bathroom of the 36
th
story,(it
has a great view! he said) and Bob got the rest of the
hotel. They even gave the hotel a name:
(and Joe in the bathroom)
And then they discovered the cheese was bouncy and
bounced their way all the way back to Earth, leaving Le Bob
Hotel behind for the next explorers to find...
The End
